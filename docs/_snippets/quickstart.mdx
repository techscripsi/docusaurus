<h1><FontAwesomeIcon icon="award" /> OmniCart Features</h1>

* **All-in-One Orders**: Seamlessly combine physical products, digital goods, one-time purchases, and subscriptions in a single cart
* **Flexible Payment Settings**: Customize payment rules using tags—set once, apply to all tagged items in the cart
* **Granular Control**: Configure settings at the cart, tag, or individual product level for maximum flexibility
* **Efficient API**: Designed for fewer requests and properties—simple scenarios
* **Advanced Subscription Management**: Use timers to automate subscription controls effortlessly
* **Easy Refunds & Confirmations**: Capture, cancel, or refund entire carts or individual items —even partial refunds are supported
